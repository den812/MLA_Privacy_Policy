## English

### 1. Overview

Shadow Pack: Card Battle ("the App") is developed by MargoLA ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Shadow Pack: Card Battle does **not** require account registration, does **not** collect personal information, and does **not** transmit any data to external servers.

---

### 2. Information We Collect

#### 2.1 Information We Do NOT Collect
- Name, email address, or any personal identifiers
- Location data (GPS or network-based)
- Device identifiers (IMEI, advertising ID)
- Photos, camera, or microphone access
- Contacts or phone data
- Payment information

#### 2.2 Information Stored Locally on Your Device
The App stores the following data **exclusively on your device** using the browser's `localStorage` mechanism:

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Game progress (coins, gems, level) | Save your game state between sessions |
| Unlocked cards | Track your collection |
| Settings (language, battle mode) | Remember your preferences |
| App version | Detect updates |

This data **never leaves your device**. We have no access to it. It is deleted when you uninstall the App.

---

### 3. Third-Party Services

The current version of Shadow Pack: Card Battle does **not** integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

Future versions may include:
- **Google AdMob** — advertising (users will be notified and this policy will be updated)
- **Google Play Billing / RevenueCat** — in-app purchases (users will be notified)

---

### 4. Children's Privacy

Shadow Pack: Card Battle is rated for ages **12 and older** (PEGI 12 / ESRB Teen). We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information through the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

---

### 5. Data Security

Since the App does not transmit or store any data on external servers, the security of your data depends solely on the security of your own device. We recommend keeping your device's operating system and security software up to date.

---

### 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this document and, where appropriate, through an in-app notice. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
