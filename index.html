About the Game

Enter the Shadow Arena

Shadow Arena: Card Battle is a strategic card game where you build your collection from 18 unique cards across three powerful clans — the Wolves, the Cats, and the Dragons. Each card has distinct attack power, health points, and a special trait that changes how it fights.

Battle opponents in 1v1 duels, embark on roguelite Shadow Runs, assemble formations to crush enemy lines, and dominate three-lane territory battles. The deeper you go, the more dangerous the arena becomes.

18 Unique Cards

Common, Rare, Epic, and Legendary rarities across Wolves, Cats, and Dragons clans.

9 Battle Traits

Berserk, Poison, Armor, Regen, Shadow, Fire, Lightning, Wind, and Shot — each with unique mechanics.

3 Mission Types

Shadow Runs, Shadow Formation, and Shadow Lanes — each a different strategic challenge.

6 Languages

Full localization in English, Russian, German, French, Spanish, and Italian.

How to Play

Master the Arena

1

Build Your Collection

Start with a set of Common cards and expand your deck by purchasing cards in the Shop with coins and gems. Rarer cards have stronger base stats and more powerful traits.

2

Choose Your Fighter

On the Select screen, swipe through your owned cards and pick the one you want to battle with. Each card brings a different playstyle to the fight.

3

Battle in 1v1 Duels

Your card fights an enemy card automatically, or switch to Manual mode mid-battle to use special actions: Fury (boost attack), Defense (reduce damage), or Shadow (disrupt the enemy).

4

Conquer Missions

Progress through three mission types for greater rewards. Missions reset daily and weekly, giving you reasons to return every day.

5

Manage Fatigue

Cards gain fatigue after battles. A fatigued card fights at reduced power. Rest cards in the Shop to keep your deck at full strength.

Mission Types

Three Paths to Glory

Shadow Arena offers three distinct mission modes, each requiring a different strategy.

Shadow Runs

A roguelite map of nodes — battles, elites, bosses, loot, and rest sites. One run, one life. How far can you go?

Shadow Formation

Place 5 cards into battle slots and fight a series of duels against an AI formation. Positioning and card synergy matter.

Shadow Lanes

Control three lanes over 7 rounds. Play cards into FLANK, CONTROL, or CROWN lanes. The player who wins the most lanes wins the match.
